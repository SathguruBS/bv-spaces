Using it well

Quick rules

  • Clear space: keep empty space around the logo at least the height of the "B" on every side.
  • Minimum size: below ~90 px / 28 mm wide, use the house icon instead of the full lockup.
  • Don't: stretch it, add shadows, recolour the letters, or place the light version on a busy photo without a dark overlay.
  • Colours: Ink #1E2A26 · Pine #2C5246 · Bronze #A9742E · Sand #E9E0CF · Paper #FAF7F0.
